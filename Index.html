The problem

That's not a small inconvenience. That's how event organizers stay stuck, overpay the wrong people, and don't scale.

📱

Your guest list lives in WhatsApp

Guests buried in 40 threads. Promoters texting names at midnight. Door person scrolling in the dark while the queue grows longer. You've been here.

🚪

The door is your slowest point

Paper lists. Flashlights. "Can you check again?" Every slow check-in costs you a guest who leaves the queue. Some nights that's hundreds of euros gone.

👥

One guest. Two entries. Wrong numbers.

The same person added by two different promoters. Now your attendance data is inflated, your capacity math is wrong, and you have no idea who's actually responsible.

💸

You pay promoters on their word

Promoter A says they brought 30. Your list says 18 actually showed. You'll never know for sure — so you guess. Over a year, that guesswork costs you thousands.

📊

Your P&L is a guess, not a number

Cash in. Expenses out. What's left? Most organizers calculate this days after the event, from memory and screenshots. By then nothing's actionable.

No system means no improvement. You can't compare last week to this week. You can't spot what's working. You're just running it again and hoping.

Duplicate guests you didn't catch. Walk-ins you didn't log. Promoter commissions you overpaid. Costs you forgot to deduct. None of this is dramatic — it just adds up, silently, every single night.

How it works

Built for the door.
Built for the morning after.

Every feature in NOX exists to answer one question: what actually happened at your event?

🚪

Door Mode — fast check-in

Dedicated full-screen check-in interface. Type a name, find a guest in under 2 seconds. Tap to mark present. Double-tap protection prevents accidental re-checks in dark, crowded entries.

🛑

Duplicate detection — automatic

When a guest is added who's already on the list — under any promoter — NOX catches it before they're in. No more inflated numbers. No more "but I swear I didn't add them twice."

💰

Live P&L — updated every tap

Every check-in updates your revenue figure. Every walk-in logged adds to cash. Subtract your known costs and your net profit is live, accurate, and actionable — before the night ends.

🏆

Promoter accountability — by the numbers

See exactly how many guests each promoter brought, how many actually showed up, and their conversion rate. Pay based on reality, not what they told you in a WhatsApp voice note.

🚶

Walk-in tracking — nothing falls through

Log every door walk-in with timestamp, entry type, and fee. Separate from the guestlist. Your total attendance count and revenue figure stay clean and accurate from open to close.

📋

Event history — compare and improve

Every event archived. Saturday vs last Saturday. This promoter vs that promoter. Capacity trends. Revenue growth. You can't improve what you don't measure.

FAQ

The questions you're already thinking.

Do I need an account to use NOX?
+
No. Open the app and start building your event — no account, no email, no credit card. The free plan saves everything locally on your device. You only need to sign up if you want cloud backup and multi-device sync (Pro).
How fast is Door Mode really?
+
Under 2 seconds for a typical check-in. The interface is full-screen, no navigation, instant search. You type 3 letters, find the guest, tap once. Double-tap protection stops accidental re-checks in dark environments. It's built for exactly the conditions your door person works in.
What exactly does duplicate detection catch?
+
When any guest is added, NOX normalises the name and checks across all promoters. It catches "alex müller" and "Alex Muller" as the same person. You get a warning and can choose to proceed or cancel. This runs on add, not just at the door — so your list stays clean before the night.
Can I use NOX for multiple events at the same time?
+
Yes. You can have as many events as you need, each fully separate. Create a new one, switch between them, or duplicate a previous event to reuse the setup. No limit on the free plan.
What if my door person and I are both using it at once?
+
Real-time multi-device use comes with Pro. On Free, you can share the device or coordinate offline. Pro gives both of you live sync so check-ins from the door instantly appear on your dashboard.
Is my data safe if I close the browser?
+
On Free, data is saved to localStorage and persists across sessions and restarts. Pro adds encrypted cloud backup so nothing is ever lost — even if your phone dies mid-event.
